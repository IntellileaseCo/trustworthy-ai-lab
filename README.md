## trustworthy-ai-lab
A public research lab exploring how AI systems can become more trustworthy, transparent and auditable.
The objective is to explore practical methods for building AI systems that are trustworthy, transparent and suitable for deployment in high-stakes, regulated environments.
# Research Areas**
- AI Oversight
- Explainability
- Evidence-Based Reasoning
- Confidence Gating
- Human-in-the-Loop Systems
- AI Governance
- Evaluation & Benchmarking
- Auditability
## Current Focus
This repository contains research notes, experiments, prototypes and technical essays investigating practical approaches to building trustworthy AI systems for high-stakes and regulated environments.
## Topics include
- Anthropic AI Safety research
- AI control and oversight
- Evidence-constrained reasoning
- Explainable decision systems
- Human review workflows
- Hallucination mitigation
- Confidence calibration
